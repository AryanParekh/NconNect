WHY DONATE?

Why to donate to a NGO to fight against Covid-19? As we know, the year 2020 did not go as many planned. The out-break of this terrifying virus, covid-19, has taken many lives and is only getting fiercer. To stop the rapid growth of the mortality rate related to covid-19, we should take an initiative by donating to NGOs who are trying to provide the proper equipment to hospitals, who are trying hard to fight against this virus.
